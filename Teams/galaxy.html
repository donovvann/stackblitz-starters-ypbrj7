Lost Galaxy Power Rangers

Overview: The Lost Galaxy Rangers, introduced in 1999, were a team of Power Rangers tasked with protecting the galaxy from the evil forces of Scorpius. Led by Leo Corbett, a former ship mechanic, the team included Maya, Damon, Kai, Kendrix, and Mike. Their adventures took place aboard the space station Terra Venture as they explored new planets and fought to protect humanity. The team encountered deadly foes and explored the mysteries of the Lost Galaxy while wielding powerful weapons and Zords.
