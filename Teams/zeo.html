Alien Power Rangers

Overview: The Zeo Rangers were the first team of Power Rangers to use the power of the Zeo Crystal, marking a new chapter in the Power Rangers saga. Introduced in 1996, the team was composed of Tommy, Kat, Adam, Tanya, and Rocky, who fought to protect Earth against the Machine Empire. Their powers were based on ancient geometric symbols, signifying increased strength and abilities compared to their previous forms.
