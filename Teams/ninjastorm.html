Ninja Storm Power Rangers

Overview: The Ninja Storm Power Rangers, introduced in 2003, are a team of skilled ninjas who must battle evil forces to protect the world. Led by Shane Clarke, the Red Ranger, the team consists of Shane, Tori, and Dustin. Their abilities come from their training in the world of ninjutsu, and they must use their powers to defeat the evil Ninja Master, Lothor, and his minions. With their incredible agility and martial arts skills, the Ninja Storm Rangers fight to protect both their world and the Ninja Academy.
