SPD Power Rangers

Overview: The SPD Power Rangers, introduced in 2005, are a team of law enforcement officers from outer space. They work in the Special Police Department (SPD), dedicated to maintaining peace in the galaxy. Led by Sky Tate, the Red Ranger, the team battles the criminal Master Org, a dangerous force threatening peace throughout the universe. The team uses advanced technology and special Ranger suits to fight against alien crime and restore order.
