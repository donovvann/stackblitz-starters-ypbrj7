Megaforce Power Rangers

Overview: The Megaforce Power Rangers, introduced in 2013, are the 20th anniversary team of Power Rangers. Led by Troy Burrows, the Red Ranger, the team is tasked with defending Earth from the evil forces of the Warstar aliens. The Megaforce Rangers must use their advanced technology and the power of the legendary Ranger powers to stop the invasion. Throughout the series, the team faces various challenges while unlocking the powers of previous Rangers, all while battling the Warstar forces and other enemies.
