Operation Overdrive Power Rangers

Overview: The Operation Overdrive Power Rangers, introduced in 2007, are a team of elite treasure hunters searching for ancient artifacts to prevent a great evil from using them. Led by Mack Hartford, the Red Ranger, the team travels the world, facing challenges while defending against the enemies and revealing secrets behind their powers and missions. The team combines their strengths and skills to protect the world from dangerous forces and restore balance.
