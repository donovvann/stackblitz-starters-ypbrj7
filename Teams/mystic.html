Mystic Force Power Rangers

Overview: The Mystic Force Power Rangers, introduced in 2006, are a team of magical warriors who fight against dark mystical forces. Led by Nick Russell, the Red Ranger, the team must use their magic and wizardry to battle the evil wizard, Imperious, and his dark army. Alongside their magical abilities, the team uses enchanted weapons and spells to protect the world. Each member has a special affinity for elemental powers, which they combine to defeat their enemies and protect the balance of magic in the universe.
