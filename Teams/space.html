space Power Rangers

Overview: The In Space Rangers, introduced in 1998, were the first team of Power Rangers to fight in space. Led by Andros, a former Earth Ranger, the team battled the evil space villain Astronema and her forces. The team included Andros, Ashley, Carlos, Cassie, and TJ, who worked together to protect the galaxy. Their adventures involved powerful space vehicles, advanced technology, and the quest to destroy the villainous forces threatening peace across the universe.
