Alien Power Rangers

Overview: The Alien Rangers, also known as the Aquitian Rangers, were introduced as a temporary replacement for the Mighty Morphin Power Rangers. Hailing from the aquatic planet Aquitar, they helped protect Earth while the original Rangers regained their powers. The team consisted of Delphine, Aurico, Cestro, Tideus, and Corcus.
