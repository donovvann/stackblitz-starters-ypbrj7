RPM Power Rangers

Overview: The RPM Power Rangers, introduced in 2009, are a post-apocalyptic team living in a world where technology has gone rogue and threatens humanity. Led by Scott Truman, the Red Ranger, the team must defend their city and restore order while battling the evil forces of the Machine Empire. Using advanced gear and high-tech Zords, the RPM Rangers fight to survive in a world that has been decimated by a war of machines and chaos.
