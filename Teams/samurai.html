Samurai Power Rangers

Overview: The Samurai Power Rangers, introduced in 2011, are a team of martial arts-trained warriors protecting the world from evil. Led by Jayden Shiba, the Red Ranger, the team must master their powers and battle the forces of Xandred, a demonic villain threatening the world. The team uses ancient Samurai symbols and high-tech suits to fight against an array of enemies and preserve the balance of the universe.
